Algebra Formulas

Algebra is a branch of mathematics that substitutes letters for numbers. An algebraic equation depicts a scale, what is done on one side of the scale with a number is also done to either side of the scale.
The numbers are constants. Algebra also includes real numbers, complex numbers, matrices, vectors and much more.
X, Y, A, B are the most commonly used letters that represent algebraic problems and equations.

List of the formulas are given below-

a 2 - b 2 = (a -b) (a + b)
(a + b)2 = a2 + 2ab + b2
